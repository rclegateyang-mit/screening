# 5. Acronyms Used — LEHD Snapshot Documentation

Source URL: https://lehd.ces.census.gov/data/lehd-snapshot-doc/S2024/appendices/acronyms.html

## Complete Acronym List

**BR** — Business Register, formerly known as the SSEL

**BRB** — Business Register Bridge

**BLS** — Bureau of Labor Statistics

**CBSA** — Core-Based Statistical Area

**CPR** — Composite Person Record. A source of residence prior to 2011.

**DRB** — Disclosure Review Board

**ECF** — Employer Characteristics File

**EHF** — Earnings History File

**EIN** — (Federal) Employer Identification Number

**ES202** — Historic reference for employer data provided for the Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages (QCEW). The "ES" mnemonic appears in variable names and data values to indicate derivation from this source.

**FIPS** — Federal Information Processing Standards

**FSRDC** — Federal Statistical Research Data Center

**FTI** — Federal Tax Information. Data derived from Internal Revenue Service (IRS) information, also referred to as Title 26 or T26.

**GAL** — Geocoded Address List

**ICF** — Individual Characteristics File. Family of tables collecting observed and imputed person-level demographic characteristics.

**J2J** — Job-To-Job Flows

**LBD** — Longitudinal Business Database

**LED** — Local Employment Dynamics. Refers to the voluntary partnership between the U.S. Census Bureau and state entities. Public use data products are considered LED products (QWI, J2J, LODES, PSEO, etc.).

**LEHD** — Longitudinal Employer Household Dynamics. Refers to the data infrastructure assembled within Census to generate LED data products. The LEHD Snapshot is a product of that infrastructure. Also refers to the program within the Center for Economic Studies at Census managing this infrastructure.

**LMI** — Labor Market Information. State government offices responsible for collecting and disseminating labor market data. Most have joined the LED partnership with Census.

**LODES** — LEHD Origin-Destination Employment Statistics. Public use data product providing information on linkages between place of residence and employment for jobs, forming microdata for the OnTheMap application.

**MAF** — Master Address File. Internal frame of residences and associated geographic characteristics.

**MAF-ARF** — Master Address File - Administrative Records File. A source of residence information in 2011.

**MAFX** — Master Address File Extract. Extract of the MAF requestable by researchers.

**MOU** — Memorandum of Understanding. Agreements between agencies governing data sharing and usage.

**MSA** — Metropolitan Statistical Area

**OPM** — Office of Personnel Management. Partners with LEHD to provide job information for federal employees, not part of the LEHD Snapshot.

**OTM** — OnTheMap. Public use LED application displaying places of work and residence for jobs.

**PIK** — Protected Identification Key. Encrypted Social Security number used within Census and at FSRDCs for linking person-level data from multiple sources.

**PSEO** — Post-Secondary Employment Outcomes. Public use LED data product reporting earnings, location, and industry of employment after post-secondary education completion.

**QCEW** — Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages. BLS public use data product with microdata collected by state LMI offices and shared with Census through LED partnership.

**QWI** — Quarterly Workforce Indicators

**RCF** — Residence Candidate File. Source for residence information after 2011.

**RDC** — Research Data Centers. Predecessor to the FSRDC system.

**SEIN** — State employer identification number. Constructed from state numeric (FIPS) code and UI account number. BLS refers to UI account number in combination with reporting unit number as SESA-ID.

**SEINUNIT** — SEIN reporting unit. The place of work (establishment) reported on the QCEW.

**SSEL** — Standard Statistical Establishment List

**SPF** — Successor-Predecessor File

**SSN** — Social Security Number

**U2W** — Unit-to-Worker Impute. Imputation procedure to assign establishments (SEINUNITs) to jobs at multi-establishment SEINs in LEHD infrastructure.

**UI** — Unemployment Insurance. In LEHD Infrastructure, UI wage records are quarterly earnings reports forming jobs data, used in state unemployment insurance system administration.

**VEO** — Veteran Employment Outcomes. Public use LED data product reporting earnings outcomes after military service.
